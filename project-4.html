South Forest Hill Residences

Parallax
Toronto, ON

Luxury Residential
High-Rise Condo

South Forest Hill Residences by Parallax Investment Corporation and Westdale Properties in Toronto introduces a new refinement to the surrounding neighbourhood. Being the first high rise condominium in this area, South Forest Hill Residences brings a sense of curated intimacy with this elevated yet timeless design. Using simplicity and clean lines throughout the project, the design comes across as elegant and luxurious, while still giving a sense of approachability to its residents.

The extensive list of amenities include wellness spa (including cold and hot plunge pools, steam, sauna, and a treatment room), children’s play room, active and private co-work zones, party room, screening room, entertainment and games lounge, fitness studio (including yoga and private training rooms), pet spa, and concierge.
