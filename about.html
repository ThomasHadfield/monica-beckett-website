As an interior designer/interior architect, I have always gravitated towards design which engages and incites interaction from its users. I enjoy questioning the canons of design and representation, while incorporating my fine art background into my designs. In order for me to have clarity of idea, I approach design as something which converts utility into behaviour, maximizing function and the human experience.
