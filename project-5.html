South Forest Hill Residences Presentation Centre

Parallax
Toronto, ON

Retail
Sales Office

The South Forest Hill Presentation Centre functions in complementary association with the South Forest Hill condominium, both in materiality and design features. Similar elements are introduced at a smaller scale, such as the concierge fireplace wall and curved ceiling baffles, in order to create a sense of place and belonging with potential purchasers. To give visitors to the sales center an inside look into our design process, custom display cases were created to showcase a few of the key amenity spaces within the condominium. These display cases exhibited a selection of curated finishes, as well as my personal hand sketches from the development phase of the project.
